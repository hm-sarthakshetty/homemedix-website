Our Story

From a small workshop to India's medical partner

Home Medix was founded in 2004 by Sukumar Shetty with a vision to bring world-class respiratory and rehabilitation devices to Indian hospitals and homes. For nearly two decades, we built deep expertise by sourcing, distributing, and servicing medical equipment across India. In 2023, we took the next step — transitioning to in-house manufacturing at our ISO 13485:2016 certified facility in Bengaluru.

Over 20 years, we've maintained one guiding principle: every device that leaves our facility must perform as reliably as the day a family depends on it most. That commitment drives our investment in precision manufacturing, rigorous QC, and a distribution model that prioritises quality and accountability at every step.

Our Mission

Clinical-grade care, accessible to all

To design, manufacture, and deliver ISO-certified medical devices that improve quality of life — making clinical-grade respiratory and rehabilitation care accessible to every Indian family and healthcare institution, regardless of geography or economic status.

We are a 100% Indian-owned, Indian-manufactured brand. The COVID-19 pandemic reinforced what we had seen for years in the field — India needed domestic manufacturing capability for critical respiratory devices. That conviction drove our transition to in-house manufacturing in 2023.

Our Facility

Where precision meets purpose

Every Home Medix device is manufactured, assembled, and quality-tested in-house at our KSSIDC Industrial Estate facility in Rajajinagar, Bengaluru — no outsourcing, no compromise.

Our assembly area for respiratory devices operates in a controlled clean-room environment, eliminating contamination risk in particle-sensitive components like nebulizer components and oxygen concentrator sieves.

Quality control that doesn't cut corners

Every batch undergoes electrical safety testing, output purity verification (oxygen concentrators), acoustic measurement (noise compliance), and mechanical endurance testing before release — with traceability records maintained for 10 years per ISO 13485 requirements.

Our Bengaluru facility maintains ready stock of all product lines for same-day and next-day dispatch to hospitals and distributors across India.

Certifications & Compliance
ISO 13485:2016

Medical Device Quality Management System — the global benchmark for medical equipment manufacturers. Covers design, development, production, installation, and servicing. Audited annually by an accredited third-party body.

ISO 9001:2016

General quality management system standard covering all aspects of our operations — procurement, manufacturing, customer service, and continuous improvement processes.

CDSCO

All Home Medix medical devices are registered with India's Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation under the Medical Devices Rules, 2017 — ensuring regulatory compliance for legal sale and supply in India.

CE Mark

Select Home Medix products carry the CE mark for export to European Economic Area markets, demonstrating conformity with EU Medical Device Regulation safety and performance requirements.
