Understanding the Device

Accurate Blood Pressure Monitoring at Home

Regular home blood pressure monitoring is one of the most effective tools for managing hypertension. It provides data between clinic visits, helps assess medication effectiveness, and catches trends that single clinic readings miss.

Oscillometric Measurement

Both models use the oscillometric method — the same technology used in hospital-grade automatic monitors. The cuff detects arterial oscillations during deflation, providing rapid and accurate readings of systolic pressure, diastolic pressure, and pulse rate.

Wide Cuff Compatibility

The 22–42 cm arm circumference range fits most adult arm sizes without needing a separate large cuff. Proper cuff fit is essential for accurate readings — a cuff that's too small will overestimate blood pressure.

Dual Power Options

Operate on 4× AAA alkaline batteries for portability, or connect via DC 5.0V power supply for continuous clinical use. Battery operation ensures readings are available even during power outages.

Who It's For

Ideal Applications

Hypertension Management

Patients on antihypertensive medication benefit from daily home monitoring to track trends, assess medication timing, and provide data to their physician between clinic visits.

Clinical Screening

OPDs, nursing stations, and pharmacy counters can use either model for quick, reliable blood pressure screening. The BPM-02's backlit display is particularly suited for dimly lit clinical environments.

Elderly & Caregiver Use

One-button operation and large display make both models accessible for elderly patients or caregivers with limited technical familiarity. The wide cuff accommodates most arm sizes without adjustment.
