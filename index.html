Our Story

Built in Bengaluru, trusted across India

Founded in 2004 at KSSIDC Industrial Estate, Rajajinagar, Home Medix India Pvt. Ltd. began by sourcing and distributing quality medical devices to Indian hospitals and families. In 2023, we transitioned to in-house manufacturing — giving us full control over quality, pricing, and supply chain.

Under the leadership of Managing Director Mr. Sukumar Shetty, we manufacture and supply precision medical equipment — backed by dual ISO certification, a dedicated biomedical service team, and over two decades of domain expertise.
