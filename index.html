Our Story

Built in Bengaluru, trusted across India

Founded in 1998 at KSSIDC Industrial Estate, Rajajinagar, HomeMedix India Pvt. Ltd. began with a simple mission: make high-quality, affordable medical devices accessible to every Indian family and healthcare facility.

Under the leadership of Managing Director Mr. Sukumar Shetty, we manufacture and supply precision medical equipment — backed by dual ISO certification, a dedicated biomedical service team, and over two decades of domain expertise.
